About us

DSH Global Logistics is a licensed freight forwarder offering air, sea, and land transport services. We are in partnership with a range of world-wide shipping companies, warehouse service providers and freight forwarders who are reputed for their competence in services and competitive rates. This enables us to provide efficient, reliable and tailored service to our clients from and to any parts of the globe.

About us

Category "A" Licensed

In cooperated in January 2022, DSH Global Logistics is a category “A” licensed freight forwarder under Director General of Merchant Shipping & Sri Lanka Ports Authority

Tailored Services

Our Services are available as separate entities, as a select combination or as a total package (one-stop solution) with the flexibility to tailor our services to suit the requirement of each individual client.

Customs Clearance & More

Among the services we provide, customs clearance, personal effects packaging, project cargo handling with supply of equipment & labour, value addition services such as Customs Guarantee cancelations, Import & Export control license, NMRA license, Petroleum, TRC, SLSI etc. are mostly picked by our clients.

Security & Confidentiality

At DSH Global Logistics, we take security and confidentiality seriously. We have implemented robust security measures to ensure the safety of all cargo entrusted to us. Our facilities are equipped with security measures to safeguard against theft, damage, or loss. Our strict confidentiality policies protect the clients' sensitive information.

Our Services

We offer a comprehensive range of logistics services to our esteemed clients, encompassing air, sea, and land transport. These services can be availed separately, in a customized combination, or as a complete package, providing a one-stop solution for all logistics needs.

International Freight

We provide international freight services for businesses looking to transport goods across borders via sea, air, or land.

Customs Clearance

We provide Customs Clearance services to help businesses manage the complex process of verifying and managing customs documentation for lawful import or export of goods.

Project Cargo

We provide specialized handling and transportation for Project Cargo, including oversized, heavy, or high-value goods, with expertise in planning, execution, and relevant regulations.

Value Added Services

We provide value-added services such as Customs Guarantee cancellations, import and export control licenses, and compliance with regulatory requirements such as NMRA, Petroleum, TRC, SLSI, etc.
